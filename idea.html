Idea

'The traditional view of the earth as a pie of a fixed size presupposes that there are only two kinds of resources in the world: raw materials and energy. But in truth there are three kinds of resources: raw materials, energy and knowledge. Raw materials and energy are exhaustible - the more you use, the less you have. Knowledge in contrast, is a growing resource - the more you use, the more you have...' Yuval Noah Harari


Global Green Buildings sources great technological discoveries that help using the finite resources more effectively and thereby reducing inefficiencies. As a first step in this endeavour, Global Green Buildings helps real estate owners and developers to understand how energy is used within buildings. With our advanced monitoring system, we see how energy in consumed while simultaneously identifying savings opportunities.


Knowing the opportunities is a great first step but having an actionable plan how to optimise a building is what will actually lead to significant energy savings. Global Green Buildings leverages its impact by using its knowledge to help large scale real estate owners becoming more efficient. With our strong set of local and global partners we have the means to effectively install new innovations that lead to exceptional energy savings.


In conclusion, Global Green Buildings has developed a concept in which large-scale enterprises are empowered to become more open, responsible and intelligent. 'Open' to the latest technological advancements in smart buildings technologies. 'Responsible' for environmental, social and governance ambitions. And 'intelligent' in the monitoring, management and reporting of large and complex energy sites. Only knowledge leads to a more effective usage of our limited resources.


Our structure supports our vision in which everyone should benefit from becoming more efficient. With our financial model no initial investments are required for real estate owners leading to a win-win-win situation.

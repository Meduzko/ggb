Voltage
as a service
Electricity is distributed through a complex network, often causing a poor power quality (overvoltages, fluctuations, harmonics). This results in higher energy consumption, higher failure rates and a reduced lifespan. Our patented systems improve the actual voltage leading to energy savings (5-15%).

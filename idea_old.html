Idea

We believe that making buildings more energy efficient has a large contribution to make the world cleaner and more harmonised. With our 4-step model we continuously optimise our own activities and use the data to benchmark other buildings and make future predictions. We use this knowledge to keep on implementing relevant solutions for our customers.


With our shared savings model we always make sure that the monthly financial contributions are less than the actual savings achieved. This way all stakeholders in the clean energy transition benefit. Our model resolves the split incentive problem between owner and tenant.


Even though we have proven the model, we feel that each day is an opportunity to learn and share new ideas, so we can all benefit from the latest technologies.


To do so, we look forward to continuing our collaborations with existing partners as well as that we welcome new partners to increase our impact and make optimal use of all relevant innovations with proven technology.


Global Green Buildings is the sales channel and market place where finance, innovations and users meet and transcend into a new era of abundance!
